Screen shots and videos

Here are some screen shots and videos that showcase the main features of Tidbits.

  • Demo video

    Here's a brief demo of Tidbits in action.

  • Home screen

    Actually, this is the only screen in Tidbits! Everything is accomplished from this very simple UI, designed for quickly searching for stuff and adding new stuff.

  • Search is king

    Tidbits in geared for searching. Just type in a word you're looking for and Tidbits will show you anything that matches, pronto.

  • Front and center!

    Those pesky cards can fall into place somewhat randomly. Double-tap a card to show it in an up close and personal style, perfect for copying and pasting or taking it easy on your tired eyes.

  • Simple to create

    Adding new tidbits is quick and easy. Just tap on the big "plus" button that appears in the upper-right corner.

  • Easy editing

    Editing existing tidbits is quick and easy, too. Tap on the "pencil in the square" icon that appears in the upper-right corner of each card.

  • Quickly load tidbits

    You can even load data from someplace else. Just format the data as a CSV (spreadsheet style) text file and you can import it into Tidbits.

  • Touchtastic on an iPad

    Tidbits works great on iPads, too. It can run as a full-screen web app, and responds beautifully to touches and taps.
