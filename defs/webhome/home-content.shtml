Tidbits is a friendly little note database.

It's like a stack of index cards. You can jot down small bits of info such as usernames, passwords, URLs, or anything you might easily forget. You then only need to remember one password — your Tidbits password — to access all your notes.

Tidbits has been designed to work in modern web browsers, including mobile browsers!
